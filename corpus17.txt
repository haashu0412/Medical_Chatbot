Overview

Initially, the flu may seem like a common cold with a runny nose, sneezing and sore throat But colds usually develop slowly, whereas the flu tends to come on suddenly And although a cold can be a nuisance, you usually feel much worse with the flu.

Complications

Pneumonia,Bronchitis,Asthma flare-ups,Heart problems,Ear infections,Pneumonia is the most serious complication For older adults and people with a chronic illness, pneumonia can be deadly.

Prevention

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) recommends annual flu vaccination for everyone age 6 months or older.
